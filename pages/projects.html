    • Study Spaces

    Campus room reservation working prototype that allows students, faculty, and staff to easily view available rooms and book them for meetings, study sessions, and other events. Users can search, favorite buildings, and view other amentities of the buildings.

    Stardew Valley Analysis

    Collects, cleans, and visualizes comprehensive data in relation to profits, XP, and other various metrics. Players can make informed decisions on optimal crops and fishing locations each season based off of these metrics.

    TS Shakespeare Classifier

    Converted lyrical text data into numerical feature vectors using a TF-IDF Vectorizer. A logistical regression model was then trained on a dataset of over 450 Taylor Swift lyrics and Shakespeare quotes, achieving 79.8% accuracy in distinguishing between them.

    Best Family Neighborhood

    Assessed the rankings of Pittsburgh neighborhoods using datasets from the Western Pennsylvania Regional Data Center. It analyzed factors like number of parks, crime rates, and school enrollment to provide an evaluation of the best neighborhood to live for families.
